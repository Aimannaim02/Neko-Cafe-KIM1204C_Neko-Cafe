Our Services

Trimming cat's claws

Nail cutting is an important element of cat grooming. We must use extreme caution when clipping the cat's nails. When trimming the cat's claws, apply mild pressure to the top of the foot and the underside pad of your cat's leg. The reason we should apply pressure to the cat's foot is to persuade it to extend its claws so that we may clip them without damaging the cat's foot.

Bathing the Cat

We brush the cat first to eliminate any unwanted hair and dirt particles before bathing it. To prevent water from entering the cat's ears, place some cotton wool in them.

Immediate towel wrap

We wrap the cats in a towel as soon as we finish washing the shampoo off the cat to keep them from becoming chilly.

Cleaning The Ears

We should make it a habit to clean the cat's ears on a regular basis using cotton wool and alcohol. To remove all of the wax and dirt particles that have accumulated in the cat's ears, use a cotton swab soaked in a moderate alcohol solution.
